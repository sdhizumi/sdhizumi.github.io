Get Right Down

2020/07/03

Inspired by KORG Gadget 2 Plugins... One day after I bought the Gadget, I already had a prototype for this song.

In the Original Mix, I have used the Gadget's synth/samplers except for the bass and some samples on Reaper. (I used Abu Dhabi, Darwin, Glasgow, Memphis, Montpellier, Lexington.)

Early Freeform Mix is a freestyle self-remix, was made with Original Mix's samples on FL Studio.

Enjoy!
